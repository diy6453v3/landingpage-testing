What does IB mean?

Introducing Broker (IB) means refers to an individual or business that introduces new customers to other companies. It is a partnership between two companies to promote business growth of both parties. In this program, IB introduces new customers to HAU, and in return, IB receives a certain amount from HAU.

How do I introduce new customers?

IB introduces customers to HAU through its website or others marketing channel. You can insert a unique link into the content and place a HAU banner on your IB website page. Customer will finish the registration by clicking on the banner.

You can also use e-mails, SNS posts, and marketing campaigns including HAU's unique links. HAU will provides everything it is necessary to help our partner, so that IB can focus on doing its best.
